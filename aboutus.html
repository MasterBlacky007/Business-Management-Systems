Master Poly (PVT) Ltd

The Polydime group is a family owned establishment associated with the Plastics industry for over 2 decades. The company has a diverse portfolio of 6 product groups processed in three factories in Sri Lanka and one in India. Employing over 600 staff, servicing the local market and export to 35 countries both directly and indirectly. Polydime is the largest processor of recycled plastic with the full value chain from waste collection to end product processing and distribution. The company has its own innovation center for product development with the future in mind
